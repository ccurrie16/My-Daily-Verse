Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Overview

My Daily Verse is a daily Bible verse app built by Cody Currie. This privacy policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address. This is used solely to identify your account and sync your saved verses across devices.

Guest Access

If you choose to continue as a guest, no personal information is collected. Guest sessions use anonymous authentication and no data is linked to your identity.

Saved Verses

Verses you save are stored in Google Firestore, linked to your account. This data is used only to provide the app's core functionality.

Notification Preferences

Your notification time preference is stored locally on your device. It is not shared with any third party.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Location data
  • Payment information
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media
  • Browsing history

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In — for authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Anonymous Authentication — used to allow guest access without an account. No personal data is collected for anonymous users.
  • Firebase / Firestore — for cloud storage of saved verses. Firebase Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
