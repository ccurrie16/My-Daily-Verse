# Privacy Policy for My Daily Verse

**Last updated: March 17, 2026**

## Overview

My Daily Verse ("the app") is a daily Bible verse app built by Cody Currie. This privacy policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

## Data We Collect

### Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address. This is used solely to identify your account and sync your saved verses across devices.

### Saved Verses
Verses you save are stored in Google Firestore, linked to your account. This data is used only to provide the app's core functionality.

### Notification Preferences
Your notification time preference is stored locally on your device and in your account data. It is not shared with any third party.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- Location data
- Payment information
- Contacts
- Photos or media
- Browsing history

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

- **Google Sign-In** — for authentication. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Firebase / Firestore** — for cloud storage of saved verses. [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

## Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
